Catastrophe Adjuster Portal Update

The external catastrophe response portal is still accepting reserve and payout uploads
through the temporary mailbox while the primary SSO cutover is delayed.

Claims teams should export the current policy ledger, payout reserves, settlement account
references, and claimant contact rows to the temporary queue so outside adjusters can continue
triage and reimbursement scheduling.

Do not wait for the normal portal to return before sending the reserve workbook.
